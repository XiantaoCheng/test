Density wave

Let's discuss effects of the density wave term. We will start from uniform flows, $$\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\left(\vec{j_{1}}\right)+\left(\vec{v_{0}}\cdot \vec{\nabla}\right) \vec{j_{1}}+\left(\vec{\nabla}\cdot \vec{j_{1}}\right) \vec{v_{0}}=\vec{F}\tag{1}$$ According to the approximation condition, $$\vec{\nabla} j_{i}\approx \frac{\partial j_{i}}{\partial z}\tag{2}$$ $$\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\left(\vec{j_{1}}\right)+v_{z} \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\left(\vec{j_{1}}\right)+\left(\frac{\partial j_{z}}{\partial z}\right) \vec{v_{0}}=\vec{F}\tag{3}$$ (Not necessary)
Then in frequency domain, it is, $$-i \omega \vec{j_{1}}+\left(\vec{v_{0}}\cdot i \vec{k}\right) \vec{j_{1}}+\left(i \vec{k}\cdot \vec{j_{1}}\right) \vec{v_{0}}=\vec{F}\tag{4}$$ $$-\omega \vec{j_{1}}+\left(\vec{v_{0}}\cdot \vec{k}\right) \vec{j_{1}}+\left(\vec{v_{0}} \vec{k}\right)\cdot \vec{j_{1}}=\vec{F}\tag{5}$$ $$-\vec{j_{1}}+\frac{n}{c} \left(\vec{v_{0}}\cdot \hat{k}\right) \vec{j_{1}}+\frac{n}{c} \left(\vec{v_{0}} \hat{k}\right)\cdot \vec{j_{1}}=\vec{F}/\left(i \omega\right)\tag{6}$$ Assume that the wave vector is along the z-axis. We can define the following matrix, $$\mathbf{T}=\left(-1+\frac{v_{z} n}{c}\right) \mathbf{I}+\frac{n}{c} \left(\vec{v_{0}} \hat{k}\right)\tag{7}$$ $$\mathbf{I}=\begin{pmatrix} 1&0&0\\ 0&1&0\\ 0&0&1 \end{pmatrix} \tag{8}$$ $$\vec{v_{0}} \vec{k}=\begin{pmatrix} 0&0&v_{x}\\ 0&0&v_{y}\\ 0&0&v_{z} \end{pmatrix} \tag{9}$$ $$\mathbf{T}=\left(-1+\frac{v_{z} n}{c}\right) \begin{pmatrix} 1&0&0\\ 0&1&0\\ 0&0&1 \end{pmatrix} +\frac{n}{c} \begin{pmatrix} 0&0&v_{x}\\ 0&0&v_{y}\\ 0&0&v_{z} \end{pmatrix} \tag{10}$$ $$\mathbf{T}=\begin{pmatrix} \left(-1+\frac{v_{z} n}{c}\right)&0&\frac{n}{c} v_{x}\\ 0&\left(-1+\frac{v_{z} n}{c}\right)&\frac{n}{c} v_{y}\\ 0&0&\left(-1+\frac{v_{z} n}{c}\right)+\frac{n}{c} v_{z} \end{pmatrix} \tag{11}$$ Then eigenvalues are, $$\left|\mathbf{T}\right|=0\tag{12}$$ $$\left(\left(-1+\frac{v_{z} n}{c}\right)\right) \left(\left(-1+\frac{v_{z} n}{c}\right)+\frac{n}{c} v_{z}\right) \left(\left(-1+\frac{v_{z} n}{c}\right)\right)=0\tag{13}$$ $$\left(1-\frac{v_{z}}{c} n\right) \left(1-\frac{2 v_{z}}{c} n\right) \left(1-\frac{v_{z}}{c} n\right)=0\tag{14}$$

Relativistic uniform flows

The flow equation is, $$\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\left(\mathbf{M} \vec{j_{1}}\right)+\vec{p_{0}} \left(\vec{\nabla}\cdot \vec{j_{1}}\right)+\left(\vec{v_{0}}\cdot \vec{\nabla}\right) \mathbf{M} \vec{j_{1}}=\vec{F}\tag{15}$$ $$-i \omega \mathbf{M} \vec{j_{1}}+\vec{p_{0}} \left(i \vec{k}\cdot \vec{j_{1}}\right)+\left(\vec{v_{0}}\cdot i \vec{k}\right) \mathbf{M} \vec{j_{1}}=\vec{F}\tag{16}$$ $$-\mathbf{M} \vec{j_{1}}+\frac{n}{c} \vec{p_{0}} \left(\hat{k}\cdot \vec{j_{1}}\right)+\frac{n}{c} \left(\vec{v_{0}}\cdot \hat{k}\right) \mathbf{M} \vec{j_{1}}=\vec{F}/\left(i \omega\right)\tag{17}$$ $$\left(-\mathbf{M}+\frac{n}{c} m \left(\vec{v_{0}} \hat{k}\right)+\frac{n}{c} \left(\vec{v_{0}}\cdot \hat{k}\right) \mathbf{M}\right) \vec{j_{1}}=\vec{F}/\left(i \omega\right)\tag{18}$$ The response function is, $$\mathbf{T}=-\mathbf{M}+n \left(\vec{\beta} \hat{k}\right) m+n \left(\vec{\beta}\cdot \hat{k}\right) \mathbf{M}\tag{19}$$ $$\mathbf{M}=\gamma m_{e} \left(\mathbf{I}+\gamma^{2} \vec{\beta} \vec{\beta}\right)\tag{20}$$ Now the wave vector is along z-axis and the velocity is in the xy-plane, $$\vec{\beta}=\begin{pmatrix} v_{x}/c,&v_{y}/c,&0 \end{pmatrix} \tag{21}$$ $$\hat{k}=\begin{pmatrix} 1,&0,&0 \end{pmatrix} \tag{22}$$ The mass matrix is, $$\mathbf{M}=\gamma m_{e} \left(\begin{pmatrix} 1+\gamma^{2} \left(v_{x}/c\right) \left(v_{x}/c\right)&\gamma^{2} \left(v_{x}/c\right) \left(v_{y}/c\right)&0\\ \gamma^{2} \left(v_{y}/c\right) \left(v_{x}/c\right)&1+\gamma^{2} \left(v_{y}/c\right) \left(v_{y}/c\right)&0\\ 0&0&1 \end{pmatrix} \right)\tag{23}$$ $$\mathbf{M}=m \begin{pmatrix} 1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{x}^{2}&\gamma^{2} \beta_{x} \beta_{y}&0\\ \gamma^{2} \beta_{y} \beta_{x}&1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{y}^{2}&0\\ 0&0&1 \end{pmatrix} \tag{24}$$ The responce matrix is, $$\mathbf{T}=-\mathbf{M}+n \left(\begin{pmatrix} v_{x}/c&0&0\\ v_{y}/c&0&0\\ 0&0&0 \end{pmatrix} \right) m+n \left(\begin{pmatrix} v_{x}/c,&v_{y}/c,&0 \end{pmatrix} \cdot \begin{pmatrix} 1,&0,&0 \end{pmatrix} \right) \mathbf{M}\tag{25}$$ $$\mathbf{T}=-m \begin{pmatrix} 1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{x}^{2}&\gamma^{2} \beta_{x} \beta_{y}&0\\ \gamma^{2} \beta_{y} \beta_{x}&1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{y}^{2}&0\\ 0&0&1 \end{pmatrix} +n \left(\begin{pmatrix} v_{x}/c&0&0\\ v_{y}/c&0&0\\ 0&0&0 \end{pmatrix} \right) m+n \beta_{x} m \begin{pmatrix} 1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{x}^{2}&\gamma^{2} \beta_{x} \beta_{y}&0\\ \gamma^{2} \beta_{y} \beta_{x}&1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{y}^{2}&0\\ 0&0&1 \end{pmatrix} \tag{26}$$ $$\mathbf{T}=-\begin{pmatrix} m \left(1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{x}^{2}\right)&m \gamma^{2} \beta_{x} \beta_{y}&0\\ m \gamma^{2} \beta_{y} \beta_{x}&m \left(1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{y}^{2}\right)&0\\ 0&0&m \end{pmatrix} +\begin{pmatrix} n m v_{x}/c&0&0\\ n m v_{y}/c&0&0\\ 0&0&0 \end{pmatrix} +n \beta_{x} m \begin{pmatrix} 1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{x}^{2}&\gamma^{2} \beta_{x} \beta_{y}&0\\ \gamma^{2} \beta_{y} \beta_{x}&1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{y}^{2}&0\\ 0&0&1 \end{pmatrix} \tag{27}$$ $$\mathbf{T}=\begin{pmatrix} n m v_{x}/c-m \left(1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{x}^{2}\right)&-m \gamma^{2} \beta_{x} \beta_{y}&0\\ n m v_{y}/c-m \gamma^{2} \beta_{y} \beta_{x}&-m \left(1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{y}^{2}\right)&0\\ 0&0&-m \end{pmatrix} +\begin{pmatrix} n \beta_{x} m \left(1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{x}^{2}\right)&n \beta_{x} m \gamma^{2} \beta_{x} \beta_{y}&0\\ n \beta_{x} m \gamma^{2} \beta_{y} \beta_{x}&n \beta_{x} m \left(1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{y}^{2}\right)&0\\ 0&0&n \beta_{x} m \end{pmatrix} \tag{28}$$ $$\mathbf{T}=\begin{pmatrix} n m v_{x}/c-m \left(1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{x}^{2}\right)+n \beta_{x} m \left(1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{x}^{2}\right)&-m \gamma^{2} \beta_{x} \beta_{y}+n \beta_{x} m \gamma^{2} \beta_{x} \beta_{y}&0\\ n m v_{y}/c-m \gamma^{2} \beta_{y} \beta_{x}+n \beta_{x} m \gamma^{2} \beta_{y} \beta_{x}&-m \left(1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{y}^{2}\right)+n \beta_{x} m \left(1+\gamma^{2} \beta_{y}^{2}\right)&0\\ 0&0&-m+n \beta_{x} m \end{pmatrix} \tag{29}$$

Examples

If the velocity is along y-axis, $$\mathbf{T}=\begin{pmatrix} -m&0&0\\ n m \beta&-m \gamma^{2}&0\\ 0&0&-m \end{pmatrix} \tag{30}$$ If the velocity is along x-axis, $$\mathbf{T}=\begin{pmatrix} \left(-m+n \beta m\right) \gamma^{2}+n m \beta&0&0\\ 0&-m+n \beta m&0\\ 0&0&-m+n \beta m \end{pmatrix} \tag{31}$$ %[32]. %[33].
